Navratri Puja – Significance, Rituals & Booking Pandit for Bangalore

Why Celebrate Navratri?

Navratri, meaning "Nine Nights," is a Hindu festival dedicated to Goddess Durga and her nine divine forms. Devotees observe fasts, perform pujas, and seek the goddess's blessings for protection, prosperity, and success. It commemorates the victory of Goddess Durga over the demon Mahishasura, symbolizing the triumph of good over evil.

Step-by-Step Navratri Puja Rituals

  • Ghatasthapana – Setting up the Kalash with sacred water
  • Durga Saptashati Path – Recitation of powerful mantras
  • Havan – Fire offering for purification
  • Kanya Pujan – Worshiping young girls as a symbol of Devi
  • Offering bhog and aarti to seek divine blessings

Benefits of Navratri Puja

  • Brings Goddess Durga’s protection and positivity
  • Enhances spiritual growth and devotion
  • Invites prosperity and success
