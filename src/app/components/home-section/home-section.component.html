Sacred Month of Savan

The fifth month of the Hindu calendar, Savan, falls usually in July and August. In the current year, the month of Savan starts from the 10th of July and continues till the 9th of August. This month is considered to be sacred and dedicated to Lord Shiva and his wife, Goddess Parvati.

Considered to be a highly auspicious and holy period of the year, a large number of people conduct several poojas during this time, like the Savan Somwar Vrats, Kanwar Yatras, Mangala Gauri Vrats, Nag Panchami Celebrations, Varalakshmi Vratam, and Savan Shivratri celebrations, among many others.
