Shubh Muhurats in January 2025

Find below the auspicious timings and reasons for performing various rituals in January 2025.

Pratipada Tithi

Date: 1st January 2025 (Wednesday)

Time: 6:23 AM to 8:45 AM

Reason: Starting the year with Puja on Shukla Paksha Pratipada is auspicious.

Sankashti Chaturthi

Date: 9th January 2025 (Thursday)

Time: Moonrise at 8:21 PM (for Chaturthi vrat puja).

Reason: Ganesha Puja on Sankashti removes obstacles.

Ekadashi Tithi

Date: 11th January 2025 (Saturday)

Time: 7:06 AM to 1:42 PM

Reason: Putrada Ekadashi is one of the most auspicious days for worshiping Lord Vishnu, which promises to be a good day for children and prosperity.

Makar Sankranti

Date: 15th January 2025 (Wednesday)

Puja Time: 7:15 AM to 12:30 PM

Reason: The transition of the Sun into Capricorn is an excellent time for Surya Puja and offering prayers for abundance.

Purnima Tithi

Date: 25th January 2025 (Saturday)

Time: 6:50 AM to 10:30 PM

Reason: Magha Purnima is considered auspicious for doing Satyanarayana Puja, Ganga Snan, and donating for the sake of attaining good karma.

Shatabhisha Nakshatra

Date: 28th January 2025 (Tuesday)

Time: 10:15 AM to 1:40 PM

Reason: Shatabhisha Nakshatra and Shukla Paksha are an auspicious time for doing all types of Puja.
