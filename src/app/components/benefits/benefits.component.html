Navratri Puja – Meaning, Rituals, and How to Book Pandit Online

Navratri is a significant Hindu festival celebrated with fervor and enthusiasm all over India. It is in honor of Goddess Durga and her nine avatars. Pujas, fasting, and seeking the Goddess's blessings for good health, prosperity, and success are all done by the devotees during these nine days.

If you wish to do Navratri Puja at home or office, booking a qualified pandit guarantees correct observance of the rituals. In this blog, we will explain why Navratri is celebrated, how to do the step-by-step process of puja, and how you can book a pandit online for Navratri puja easily.

Why Celebrate Navratri?

Navratri is "Nine Nights" and is devoted to Goddess Durga, who symbolizes strength, protection, and victory over evil. It is said that during Navratri, Goddess Durga battled the demon Mahishasura and vanquished him on the tenth day, which is Vijayadashami (Dussehra).

Every day of Navratri is devoted to a different incarnation of Maa Durga, and devotees worship her with various colors and offerings.
