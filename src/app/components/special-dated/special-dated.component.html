🚫 No Auspicious Marriage Dates

There are no auspicious marriage muhurats in July, August, September, and October 2025 since Lord Vishnu enters Yoga Nidra (divine sleep). This period is known as Chaturmas, during which auspicious ceremonies like weddings are generally avoided.

✅ However, auspicious marriage dates resume in November and December 2025.
