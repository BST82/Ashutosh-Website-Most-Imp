Our Services

Wedding Services

  • Shubh Vivah: This traditional Hindu wedding ceremony includes all the key rituals such as Kanyadaan, Saptapadi, Sindoor Daan, and more, ensuring a spiritual and blissful union.
  • Arya Samaj Marriage: If you are looking for a simple, non-extravagant, yet meaningful wedding, our Arya Samaj pandit service is perfect for you. Arya Samaj weddings are based on Vedic principles, focusing on simplicity and the essence of the sacred union.
  • Engagement Ceremony: For pre-wedding rituals like the engagement or Roka ceremony, our pandits will conduct the ceremony with all the traditional practices, making it a special and sacred event.

Pandit for Grihpravesh and Havan

Moving into a new home or office is a significant moment in life. We provide pandits for Grihpravesh and havan, guiding you through ceremonies with proper Vedic procedures.

  • Grihpravesh Puja: Our pandits perform the ritual to purify the space and bring positive energy and blessings to your household.
  • Vastu Shanti Puja: Performed after construction or renovation to align the space with positive cosmic forces and remove negative influences.
  • Mundan Sanskar (First Haircut Ceremony): This important rite purifies the child's body and soul, ensuring their well-being.
  • Havan: A sacred fire ceremony for purifying the environment and warding off negative energies.

Pandit for Last Rites (Antim Sanskar)

In difficult times, our pandits provide compassionate and respectful support, guiding you through the last rites with dignity and adherence to Hindu customs.

  • We assist with all necessary rituals from Pind Daan to Asthi Visarjan, ensuring a respectful farewell.

Pandit for Katha and Jaap

For spiritual growth, we offer pandits for Katha and Jaap to guide you through spiritual narrations and chanting rituals.

  • Ram Katha: A powerful narration of Lord Rama’s life and teachings, bringing peace and harmony to your household.
  • Bhagwat Katha: A spiritual narration of Lord Krishna’s teachings, deepening your connection with spirituality.
  • Maha Mrityunjaya Jaap: This mantra Jaap is for health, long life, and protection from untimely death.
