  • Marriage, wedding pujaCommon puja and vidhi are performed in Hindu marriages.

    Ganesh puja

    It gives a shubharambh to worship Lord Ganesh for the blessing of Lord Ganesh and to remove hurdles for the future.

    Kalash sthapana and navgrah puja

    For hurdle, tackle invoke nine planets for positivity established Kalash and perform puja for nine planets.

    Kanyadaan

    This is the most sacred ritual, in which the parent gives the daughter's hand to the groom while chanting a mantra, while the father provides a hand to the groom.

    Vivah havan

    To seek the blessing of the agni devta couple walks around the sacred fire and takes 7 vows pheras pandit chanting the mantra during pheras.

    Mangalsutra and Sindur ceremony

    The groom ties the mangal sutra to the bride's neck and applies sindoor to her forehead maang

    Ashirwad and vidai

    The couple touches the elder's and parents' feet and takes blessings vidai the emotional part of the ceremony, where the daughter goes with the groom. Everyone is emotional in this ceremony.

  • Grihapravesh pujaCleansing the home to remove all negativity and seeking blessing from gods ensuring protection from bad energies.

    Types of grihpravesh

    Apoorva Grihpravesh: for moving into a completely new home Sapoorva grihpravesh: puja after renovation and repair Dwandwah grihpravesh: moving back home after any bad event

    Puja for grihpravesh

    Ganesh puja, navgraha puja, Kalash sthapana, havan, milk boiling ritual, pravesh entering into the house, Vastu shanti

    Benefits of grihpravesh puja: removes negativity and gives positivity

  • Satyanarayan Katha

    Benefits

    For seeking lord Vishnu's blessing for success in life and positivity and for fulfilling wishes It is good for family members and brings happiness.

    On full moon days purnima, Ekadashi, Sankranti, weddings, birthdays, and any special occasions

    sankalp , ganesh puja, kalash sthapana, vishnu puja, satyanarayan katha, havan, aarti, prasad vitran,

    Lord Vishnu picture, Kalash, tulsi leaves, fruits, flowers, panchamrit, sweets, puja thali, diya, Kapoor, matchbox, havan samgri, ghee.

  • Shradh and pitra pujaIt is a Hindu ritual performed during the pitra paks and punyatithi for our departed ancestors' purvaj.During this time, the souls of our ancestors come to the earth, and by performing shradh puja, they give their blessing, and we ensure their moksha peace.

    Benefit

    Poorvaj mooksh(ancestor peace), family positivity, growth.

    Puja date time

    Our ancestor passed away on the 15th day of the sacred Pitra Paksha in the Hindu month of Bhadrapada, which is their tithi.

    Puja vidhi

    Sankalp, remember ancestors, pinda daan, tarpan, havan, feeding brahmins and cows, dogs, crow, offering food to poorvaj,

    Samagri items

    Kala til, jwar, chawal, kusha, pinada, flowers, fruits, matchbox, havan samagri, ghee, kapoor, firewood, cloths, diya, etc.

    Guideline

    Avoid any kind of celebration new venture subh kaam.
